# HP Bars

> **FREE** - All HP bar styling is included at no extra cost

Customize health bars to match your brand and game style.

## What Are HP Bars?

HP (Health Points) bars show player or enemy health status. They fill and deplete as damage is taken or health is restored.

## What You Can Customize

| Property | What It Does |
|----------|--------------|
| Main Color | The health fill color |
| Indicator Color | Damage flash or secondary color |
| Background Color | The empty bar background |
| Border | Outline around the bar |

## Customizing HP Bars

1. Find HP bar settings in the dashboard
2. Select which color to change
3. Choose your color
4. Preview the bar at different fill levels
5. Save

## HP Bar Anatomy

```
[██████████░░░░░░░░░░] 50% HP
 ^         ^
 |         |
 Fill      Empty (background)
```

* **Fill** - Shows remaining health (main color)
* **Empty** - Shows lost health (background color)
* **Indicator** - Flashes when damage taken

## Best Practices

### Color Choices

* **Main health** - Green or your brand color
* **Damage** - Red or orange for visibility
* **Background** - Dark, subtle color

### Visual Clarity

* Clear contrast between full and empty
* Easy to read at a glance
* Matches overall game aesthetic

## Why Can't I Change HP Values?

HP bars show health controlled by the game:

| You CAN change | You CANNOT change |
|----------------|-------------------|
| Bar colors | Maximum HP amount |
| Bar style | Damage amounts |
| Visual effects | Healing amounts |

The numbers are determined by game logic. See [Game Settings](../game-settings/difficulty.md) for gameplay value adjustments.
