# Accessing the Dashboard

> **FREE** - Dashboard access is included at no extra cost

This guide will help you log in and access your Parameter Dashboard.

## Getting Your Login Credentials

Your account manager will provide you with:

* **Dashboard URL** - The web address to access your dashboard
* **Email** - Your login email address
* **Password** - Your initial password (you can change this later)

Don't have credentials yet? Contact your account manager to get set up.

## Logging In

1. **Open your browser** - Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge all work great
2. **Go to the dashboard URL** provided by your account manager
3. **Enter your email** in the login field
4. **Enter your password**
5. **Click "Sign In"**

## First Time Login Tips

* **Change your password** after your first login for security
* **Bookmark the URL** so you can find it easily next time
* **Use a strong password** - mix letters, numbers, and symbols

## Forgot Your Password?

1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for the reset link
4. Follow the link to create a new password

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Can't find the URL | Contact your account manager |
| Password doesn't work | Use "Forgot Password" to reset |
| Account locked | Wait 15 minutes, then try again |
| No email received | Check spam folder, then contact support |
