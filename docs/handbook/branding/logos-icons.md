# Logos & Icons

> **FREE** - All logo and icon changes are included at no extra cost

Make your playable ad unmistakably yours with your brand's logo and icons.

## Replacing Your Logo

Your logo is one of the most important branding elements. Here's how to update it:

1. Find the logo element in the dashboard
2. Click "Upload" or "Replace"
3. Select your logo file
4. Check the preview
5. Save your changes

## Logo Requirements

For best results, use a logo that:

| Requirement | Recommendation |
|-------------|----------------|
| Size | 512 x 512 pixels |
| Format | PNG with transparent background |
| File size | Under 500KB |
| Background | Transparent (no white box) |
| Style | Works on both light and dark backgrounds |

## Preparing Your Logo

### Remove the Background

If your logo has a white or colored background:

1. Use a design tool to remove the background
2. Export as PNG (preserves transparency)
3. Check that edges are clean

### Size It Right

* Too small = pixelated and blurry
* Too large = slow loading, wasted bandwidth
* Just right = 512x512 for logos, 256x256 for icons

## App Icons

App icons (like store icons) have similar requirements:

| Property | Specification |
|----------|---------------|
| Size | 256 x 256 pixels |
| Format | PNG |
| Shape | Square (rounded corners applied automatically) |
| Background | Can be solid color or transparent |

## Tips for Great Logos

* **Use your official logo** - The one from your brand guidelines
* **Keep it simple** - Detailed logos may not show well at small sizes
* **Test visibility** - Make sure it's visible on the ad's background
* **Consider contrast** - Light logo on dark background, or dark on light

## Common Logo Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| White box around logo | Export with transparent background |
| Logo too small/blurry | Use minimum 512x512 source |
| Colors look wrong | Use sRGB color profile |
| Logo hard to see | Add outline or adjust background |
